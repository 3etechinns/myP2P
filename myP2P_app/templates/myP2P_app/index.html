Why myP2P?

myP2P connects borrowers with investors through a proprietary matching algorithm. Loans offered to consumers are made by other consumers, who are drawn to myP2P because of its higher returns compared to a typical bank loan.

Pay Off Credit Card Debt

Pay off your credit card debt with a loan and save money with an affordable, fixed interest rate.

Make Home Improvements

As opposed to traditional bank loans, myP2P home loans are based solely on your credit worthiness, and NOT the value of your house. This will give you the flexiblity you deserve.

Cover Major Expenses

Whether it's a new car, TV or furniture, myP2P provides great financing options.

Debt Consolidation

Pay off multiple bills in a single and simple payment.

How to Borrow

Fill out a short form with your personal details as well as the desired amount you would like to borrow.

Select the monthly payment and interest rate option that fits you best.

Sit back, relax and let us do the work in depositing the money into your account.

How to Lend

The first step to becoming an investor is opening an account. Browse our options for the type of account that best suits you.

Pick an investment strategy. This can be a preselected strategy based on what we offer, or you may choose to create your own.

Connect your bank account to your myP2P account. Your earnings will be automatically deposited into your account.
