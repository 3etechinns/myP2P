Borrowing FAQ

How long does it typically take to get a loan?

Usually about 7 days, though in certain cases it may take a little longer.

What determines my credit score?

Once you apply for a loan, a credit inquiry will be created so we can best evaluate your credit worthiness. Don't worry, no other creditors will be able to access your credit report as this is strictly confidential.

What happens once I qualify for a loan?

You'll receive multiple loan offers - each showing the loan amount, APR, interest rate, monthly payment, and term.

What information do I need to provide?

After registering we'll ask you to provide the following in a separate form: Social Security, current income and employment history.

Investing FAQ

Are there retirement accounts?

Yes - we offer Traditional IRA, Roth IRA and 401(k) Rollover accounts.

What investment account options do I have?

Individual account, joint account, trust account, corporate account and custodial/minor account.

What is your most popular account?

Standard individual investment accounts are our most popular.

Is the risk of each loan being accounted for?

Yes - Loans are graded from A-E (A being lowest risk) with average historical returns ranging from 4.5%-6.75%.
